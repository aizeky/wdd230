Target Audience

The Target audience of this website are people who would like easy access to information about the weather. The website, Dodge the Cold, is for people who want to be prepared for extreme cold weathers. The website will give suggestions on what they should prepare for according to weather. It will suggest that how warm they should dress up for the weather, or just stay at home and be lazy.

Personas

1st Persona

The first person is Nathan, he is 26 years old and he is a company manager that works from home due to the pandemic. He is currently dating and is getting ready to get married and start his own family. This worker plans to schedule a meeting with his or her team the next day. He plans his day out using his laptop, but can access all of that information through his smartphone.

Scenario

What's the weather going to be like today? I need to go to work?
What's the best time to go to work tomorrow to dodge the snow?
Do I need to wear a jacket tomorrow? Will it be windy?
How long will it snow tomorrow? Do I need to wear boots?
Do I need to get really earlier to defreeze my car?
